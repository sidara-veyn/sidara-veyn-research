## 2. Abstract

Provide a concise summary of the proposal:

* what is being proposed
* why it is necessary
* expected system impact
* key risks and benefits



## 3. Motivation

Explain the rationale behind the proposal.

Include:

* current system limitations
* observed inefficiencies or risks
* governance or protocol gaps
* justification for change



## 4. System Context

Describe where this proposal applies within the system.

Include:

* affected modules (governance / coordination / execution / knowledge)
* impacted protocols or subsystems
* dependency relationships



## 5. Specification
